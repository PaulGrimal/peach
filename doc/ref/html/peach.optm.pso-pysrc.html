 11  Basic Particle Swarm Optimization (PSO) 
 12   
 13  This sub-package implements traditional particle swarm optimizers as described 
 14  in literature. It consists of a very simple algorithm emulating the behaviour 
 15  of a flock of birds (though in a very simplified way). A population of particles 
 16  is created, each particle with its corresponding velocity. They fly towards the 
 17  particle and local best, thus exploring the whole domain. 
 18   
 19  Within this implementation, it is possible to specify the ranges of search, 
 20  which will influence the max velocity of the particles, and the population size. 
 21  Other parameters are available too, please refer to the rest of this 
 22  documentation for further details. 
 23   
 24  For efficiency purposes, the particles are represented internally as an array of 
 25  vectors. The particles can be acessed externally by the corresponding propery. 
 26  See below. 
 27  """
